# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: January 27, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how "Exporter tool for X (Twitter)" (the "Extension") handles your data.

**1. Data Collection**
We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to our servers. All data processing happens locally on your device.

**2. Data Usage**
The Extension only accesses data visible on the web pages you visit (specifically x.com and twitter.com) for the sole purpose of generating the CSV export file that you request. This data is saved directly to your computer's downloads folder.

**3. Third-Party Services**
We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.

**4. Contact**
If you have any questions, please contact us via the Chrome Web Store support page.
