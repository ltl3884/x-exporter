Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 28, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how X Exporter ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with your use of our browser extension and website. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure experience.

2. Information We Collect

X Exporter is designed with a "Privacy First" approach.

  • No Personal Data Collection (Extension): We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, X (Twitter) account credentials, or browsing history to our servers via the browser extension.
  • Website Analytics (Landing Page): While the extension operates locally, our landing page (this website) uses Google Analytics to understand visitor trends. This collects anonymous data such as page views, regional location, and referral sources. This data is not linked to your extension usage data.
  • Local Processing: All data extraction and processing happen entirely within your local browser environment. The data you export (Followers, Following, etc.) is saved directly to your device as a CSV file.

3. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any information, we do not use, share, or sell any user data. The extension only uses the data it "sees" on your active X session to generate the requested export file for your personal use.

4. Security

We employ industry-standard practices to ensure the extension operates safely. By running 100% locally and not requiring any API keys or OAuth permissions, we eliminate the risk of remote data breaches.

5. Third-Party Services

Our extension interacts with X.com. Your use of X.com is governed by X Corp's own Privacy Policy. We are not affiliated with X Corp.

6. Children's Privacy

X Exporter does not knowingly collect information from children. Our services are not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our official support channels.
